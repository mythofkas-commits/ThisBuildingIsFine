# M4.1 Corrective Patch - Extraction Interaction

Date: 2026-05-16

## Issue

Manual M4 playtest found that after all three Incident Reports were collected, standing at the elevator showed vague HUD text but did not reliably trigger the extraction/win state.

## Cause

M4 used a passive proximity completion path and the HUD said to stand professionally. The smoke test teleported directly into the zone and accepted automatic completion, so it did not prove the player-facing completion action was clear during normal play.

## Fix

- Kept extraction locked until all required Incident Reports are collected.
- Added a clear approved-extraction prompt: `Press E to file audit.`
- Changed active extraction to complete only when the player is near the approved elevator and presses `E`.
- Preserved restart reset for reports, extraction availability, win state, HUD, and player position.
- Updated smoke proof to verify locked extraction, unlocked prompt, `E` completion, and restart reset.
