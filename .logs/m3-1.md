# M3.1 - Signage Layout Cleanup

Date: 2026-05-16

## Scope

Corrective M3 patch only. Fixed oversized/obstructive signage placement while preserving Incident Report collection, HUD report count/status, restart reset, movement, collision, and five-room traversal. Did not add extraction, Clarity, Performance Review, The Meeting, AI assets, enemies, combat, inventory, or multiplayer.

## Cause

The records room reused the generic room-label placement on the south wall. For records, the south wall is also the doorway from the cubicles room, so the `RECORDS REMEMBER` label appeared low and centered near the traversal/camera path. The secondary records sign was also placed on the same south-wall plane, compounding the visual obstruction.

## Fix

- Reduced default wall-sign dimensions from large face-height panels to smaller decorative office signage.
- Raised room labels above normal first-person camera height.
- Moved room labels away from doorway walls when the preferred wall contains an exit.
- Moved `records-label` to the records west wall at `(4.08, 2.28, 8.0)`.
- Moved `records-sign` to the records north wall at `(8.0, 2.34, 11.78)`.
- Kept decorative signs and posters intentionally non-collidable.
- Added smoke proof camera positioning for records-room signage screenshots only.
