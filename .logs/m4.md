## Implementation Notes

- Added `elevator-extraction` in the elevator room.
- Extraction remains locked until all 3 Incident Reports are collected.
- Locked extraction status: "Extraction unavailable. The elevator is waiting for adequate paperwork."
- Available extraction status: "Extraction approved. Please leave before the building reconsiders."
- Win status: "Audit filed. The building has marked your concerns as partially received."
- Restart resets collected reports, extraction availability, win state, HUD text, and player position.
- Extraction visual state uses a small procedural floor marker and status sign; both are non-collidable.
