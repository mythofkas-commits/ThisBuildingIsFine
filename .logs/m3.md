## Implementation Notes

- Replaced the single M2 placeholder report coupling with three source-defined reports:
  - `IR-01` in `lobby` at `(-1.2, 1.02, -0.4)`.
  - `IR-02` in `cubicles` at `(9.45, 1.0, 0.8)`.
  - `IR-03` in `elevator` at `(13.9, 1.0, 6.3)`.
- Reports are procedural Babylon planes with DynamicTexture labels.
- Reports are intentionally non-collidable, while walls and hard office props keep M2.1 AABB collision.
- Collection is proximity-based and updates `GameState`.
- HUD now shows `Incident Reports: collected / total` and a nearest pending report hint.
- Restart clears collected IDs, restores report visibility, resets player position, and preserves existing restart flow.
