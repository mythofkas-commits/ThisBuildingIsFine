# M7 - The Meeting Hazard

Date: 2026-05-16

## Summary

Added The Meeting as a source-driven conference-room spatial hazard. It is an event, not a creature: entering the conference-room Meeting zone causes procedural chairs, a side table, and an agenda panel to rearrange into a partial pressure shape while leaving escape paths open.

## Behavior

- Trigger zone: conference room, centered at `x=16, z=0`, radius `3.25`.
- Activation: entering the zone starts `assembling`, emits dry Meeting status/narrator text, and moves Meeting objects from wall-staged idle positions toward the partial ring.
- Pressure: Meeting objects are registered as dynamic collision blockers, so the arrangement is spatially relevant.
- Escape: leaving the zone switches the hazard to `cooling`, emits "The meeting has failed to reach you.", and moves objects back to idle.
- Clarity: staying inside long enough applies one subtle `meeting-exposure` Clarity event for `-3`.
- Restart: resets Meeting state and object positions.
