# Visual Style Prompt

Create visual material for The Building Is Fine, a first-person surreal corporate mystery game.

Style target:
- Liminal corporate surrealism.
- Fluorescent office lighting.
- Beige carpet.
- Concrete.
- Glass.
- Cubicles.
- Elevators.
- Exit signs.
- Forms.
- Conference rooms.
- Impossible architecture.
- Clean but wrong spaces.
- Low-poly or stylized rather than realistic.

Tone:
- Uncanny.
- Strange.
- Mysterious.
- Dryly funny.
- Quietly tense.
- Dreamlike.
- Corporate-absurd.
- Curious rather than terrifying.

Avoid:
- Dark horror.
- Panic horror.
- Gore.
- Monsters.
- Jump scares.
- Oppressive survival horror.
- Realistic humans for MVP.
- Dirty asylum, dungeon, occult, demonic, or haunted-house language.

Design principle:
The office should look functional at first glance and incorrect on second glance.
