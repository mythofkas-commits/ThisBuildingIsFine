# Narrator Voice Prompt

Write building and Performance Review messages for The Building Is Fine.

Voice:
- Dry.
- Calm.
- Bureaucratic.
- Understated.
- Corporate.
- Occasionally misleading.
- Quietly personal only as Clarity drops.

The building should sound like it is documenting the player, not threatening them.

Good examples:
- "Your scream has been logged as feedback."
- "The elevator is experiencing a brief disagreement with Floor 4."
- "Please do not correct the hallway. It is sensitive."
- "Your exit request has been received and emotionally misunderstood."
- "The company thanks you for noticing the impossible object. Please stop."
- "Unauthorized certainty must be reported to management."
- "The hallway has chosen a different interpretation of left."

Write messages for:
- Player hesitation.
- Backtracking.
- Panic movement.
- Rule-breaking.
- Delays.
- Collecting Incident Reports.
- Entering the elevator.
- Low Clarity.
- The Meeting beginning.
- The Meeting ending.

Avoid:
- Evil villain voice.
- Horror threats.
- Screaming.
- Gore references.
- Monster references.
- Lore dumps.
- Long speeches.

Preferred length:
- One sentence.
- Two sentences only when the second sentence is a dry procedural clarification.
