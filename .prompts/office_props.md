# Office Props Prompt

Generate or describe office props for The Building Is Fine.

Useful MVP props:
- Incident Reports.
- Clipboards.
- Filing cabinets.
- Desk phones.
- Projector screens.
- Conference tables.
- Stackable chairs.
- Rolling office chairs.
- Cubicle partitions.
- Fluorescent light panels.
- Exit signs.
- Department signs.
- Badges.
- Forms.
- Portraits without realistic people.
- Water coolers.
- Copy machines.

Style:
- Stylized, low-poly, readable silhouettes.
- Corporate-neutral colors with small surreal inconsistencies.
- Clean enough to avoid horror.
- Slightly too orderly or too compliant.

Interaction needs:
- Incident Reports must be recognizable as collectibles.
- Conference objects must support The Meeting hazard.
- Signs must support Clarity contradictions.
- Elevator props must support extraction.

Avoid:
- Realistic human faces.
- Weapons.
- Gore.
- Horror props.
- Clutter that blocks navigation.
- High-detail assets requiring manual cleanup.
